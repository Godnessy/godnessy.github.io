The point of this game is reaching 100 points faster than the other player,
You gather points by pressing roll dice, this will roll the dice and the value of the dice rolled will add to your current score.
If you roll a 1 your score will be zeroed and the turn will move to the other player.
If you are finished with your turn and wish to hold the value you gathered press HOLD and the current score will be added to your total score. Good luck!
